Mini Story
Scene 1
별소년은 혼자 무서웠어요.
The star-boy was all alone and scared.
"미래가 무서워..."
"The future scares me..."
Scene 2
그때 깜찍 여우가 나타났어요!
Just then a cute fox suddenly appeared!
Scene 3
여우가 말했어요.
The fox said:
"어서 와!"
"Come quickly!"
Scene 4
별소년이 말했어요.
The star-boy said:
"기다렸어! 아무 걱정 하지 마! 잘 될 거야!"
"I've been waiting! Don't worry at all! It'll all work out!"
Scene 5
둘이 함께 빛났어요! ✨
Together, they shone brighter than ever!
