Step 2

⚖️ Grammar — ~됐건

📚 whether it's ~ or ~ — no matter what!
~됐건  =  "whether it's ~ or ~"
Use when something doesn't matter either way

💡 How to make it:  noun + 이/가 됐건  |  verb stem + 됐건

🎧 From the song
낮이 됐건 밤이 됐건
Whether it's day or night
의심 없이 I love you
— without a doubt, I love you
📚 Everyday example
빵이 됐건 밥이 됐건
Whether it's bread or rice
빨리 줘. 배고파!
— just give it to me, I'm hungry!
💬 Casual speech
좋건 싫건 해야 해
Whether you like it or not
임마, 해야 해.
— dude, you just gotta do it.
🎧 In the song: '됐건' (standard) — in spoken Korean you'll often hear '되든' instead!  빵이 되든 밥이 되든~
Step 3

⚖️ Grammar — ~을수록

📚 the more ~ the more ~!
~을수록  =  "the more ~, the more ~"
When one thing increases, another changes too

💡 How to make it:  verb stem + (으)면 + stem + 을수록  |  no 받침 → ㄹ수록

🎧 From the song
원하면 원할수록
The more I want, the more~
I feel so lonely
— from the song
📚 Everyday example
공부하면 할수록 어려워
The more you study, the harder
그래도 계속해야지!
— but you've gotta keep going!
💬 Casual speech
볼수록 좋아져
The more I see you, the more I like you
큰일났어 ㅋㅋ
— I'm so in trouble lol
⚖️ Key point: A하면 A할수록 = repeated emphasis!  원하면 원할수록 = the more he wants, the MORE he wants

Step 5

💬 Real Conversation Practice

Read the situation and respond using ~됐건 or ~을수록!

👥 Between friends
💡 Use: ~됐건 + ~을수록
친구 Friend:
"한국어 공부 힘들지 않아? 그냥 포기할까봐..."
Isn't studying Korean hard? I feel like giving up...
나 Me:
"힘들건 쉽건 상관없어!  공부할수록 실력은 느는 거잖아~"
Whether it's hard or easy — the more you study, the better you get!
💼 At work
💡 Use: ~됐건
상사 Boss:
"이번 프로젝트 진행하기 좀 어려운 상황인데, 계속 맡아줄 수 있어요?"
This project is in a tough spot — can you keep handling it?
나 Me:
"상황이 어떻게 되든 끝까지 책임지겠습니다."
Whatever the situation, I'll see it through to the end.
🎧 Talking about ENHYPEN
💡 Use: ~을수록
친구 Friend:
"엔하이픈 어때? 처음엔 별로였다고 했잖아."
What do you think of ENHYPEN? You said you weren't into them at first.
나 Me:
"볼수록 좋아지는 것 같아... 큰일났어 ㅋㅋ"
The more I see them, the more I like them... I'm in trouble lol
