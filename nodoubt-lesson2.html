Step 3

🗣️ Pattern Drill

Use the words to make sentences!

나는 (지금)          !
Q. How are you feeling lately?  요즘 어때요?
🔊 나는 (지금) 불안해!
I'm anxious right now!
Q. Why is it hard?  왜 힘들어요?
🔊 나는 (지금) 괴로워!
I'm in pain!
Q. How do you feel alone?  혼자 있을 때 어때요?
🔊 나는 (지금) 외로워!
I'm lonely!
Q. What do you want?  뭘 원해요?
🔊 나는 ___ 원해!
I want ___!
Step 4

📚 Grammar — ~지만

but / however — when two things contradict each other!
~지만  =  ~ but / ~ however
Use when the second part is opposite or unexpected
🎧 From the song
널 원해  괴롭지만
neol won-hae  goerop-ji-man
I want you, but it's painful
📚 Example
한국어가 어렵지만  재미있어요
han-gu-geo-ga eo-ryeop-ji-man
Korean is hard, but it's fun
💙 Example
외로워  하지만  괜찮아
we-ro-wo  ha-ji-man  gwaen-cha-na
I'm lonely, but I'm okay
