The Story of the Korean Alphabet
한글 Hangul
Created in 1443 · One of the world's most scientific writing systems
👑
King Sejong the Great (세종대왕)
In 1443, King Sejong created Hangul so everyone could read and write. "A wise man can acquaint himself with them before the morning is over."
💡 Hangul has only 14 consonants and 10 vowels — just 24 characters. Most people can learn to read Hangul in a single afternoon!
